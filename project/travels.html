IRELAND

I went to Ireland for no good reason when I was 20. I just wanted to leave home and see what’s up. I started getting small jobs, first time working in a call centre, first time working at a hostel, that kinda things. And at that time even small jobs paid decently there so I spent a decent amount of time checking out the countryside, and an indecent amount of times in bars naturally.
