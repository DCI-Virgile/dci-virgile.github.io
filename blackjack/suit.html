Players:

Players in BlackJack Network play as space bounty hunters. Within the Network, Cowboys are given a rating in the Action Suits, Spade, Heart and Club. Action Suits represent what a Cowboy is good at.

Game Master:

The Game Master in BlackJack Network takes up the role of the liaison agent. Liaison agents are given a ranking outside the Action Suits called Diamond ranking. Every bounty has a Diamond rank, representing both the difficulty and how well it pays.
