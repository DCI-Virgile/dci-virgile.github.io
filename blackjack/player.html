Pick your strong Suit:

♠♥♣

You have also the option not to select a strong suit. It would make you a Jack of all Trades, with the advantage of being more adaptable and progressing a little bit quicker at the beginning.



The Spade suit is used for actions that require to execute something carefully planned, to stay cool under fire. It's the action suit for infiltrators, hackers and "professionals".

The Heart suit is used for actions that require social finesse, seduction or persuasion. It's the action suit of con artists and smooth operators.

The Club suit is used for actions that require force. It's the action suits of gunslingers, brutes and athletes.
